Operating principles

Pivot around what is surely real

The coherent center is not “fully personalized AI civilization in a box.” It is a compiler from intake to PersonalBrief: typed, inspectable, revisable, and narrow enough to trust.

1

Compile, don’t cosplay

Cards, motifs, and ritual matter only when they terminate in real parameters, policies, and implementation choices.

2

Inference stays revisable

Every hypothesis should be evidence-bearing, confidence-scored, and open to user correction instead of hidden psychologizing.

3

One real artifact first

The current product center is the brief: portrait, archetypes, capabilities, contract, and implementation path.

4

Resilience over theatrics

If providers fail, the system still emits structurally valid output. Graceful degradation is part of the product.

5

Frontstage and backstage stay distinct

Practical constraints, behavioral preferences, and symbolic priors are separate strata that merge only in compilation.

6

Build quality follows brief quality

Deployment, agents, and custom interfaces matter later. The design brief must become coherent before automation expands.

Process

How Ἑυνοῦς works

The product language is hylomorphic, but the practical target is tighter: raw intake enters as material, the system records a cut through it, and the residue is a buildable brief rather than a vague promise of total personalization.

1. Receive the grain

Requests begin rough: snippets, logs, half-formed questions, mismatched constraints, a pile of matter.

2. Cut a line

The system finds the axis of the task and makes the reasoning legible. The grain line becomes the trace of that cut.

3. Ignite the core

The ember pulse marks transformation: rough material turns into a bounded output with momentum and heat.

4. Leave a durable brief

The ash layer signals persistence. The result should be reusable, inspectable, and strong enough to drive later implementation.
